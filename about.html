WHO WE ARE

Established in 1972,
IFM is the oldest higher learning financial institution in Tanzania.

IFM is a hub for teaching, research, and consultancy. Currently, the Institute enrolls about 8,400 students in undergraduate and graduate programmes   who are challenged and inspired to investigate critical 21st century issues in areas of modern financial management, insurance, social protection, information technology and computer science. Since it's establishment, the Institute has had a staggering 13,652 alumni.

Our Mission :

Endeavour to provide quality academic and professional training that nurture creativity and innovation through integrated training, research and consultancy in financial studies and related disciplines.

Our Vision :

To become a world class centre of academic and professional excellence and agent for change in financial studies and related disciplines.


Our Core Values

  • Customer Focus Being customer-driven in all endeavours, sensitive and responsive to customer needs, care and satisfaction
  • Integrity Exercising high fidelity, honest, faithful and trustworthy; professional, and having IFM’s best interest at heart
  • Fairness Treating people equally; demonstrating objectivity; observing, upholding and practicing justice in all matters.
  • Accountability Being responsible, diligent and ethical in decision making, transactions, dealings and performances
  • Teamwork Commonality of purpose and unity of direction; avoiding conflicts and divisiveness and acting like a family
  • Competence Being knowledgeable, resourceful and professional in conduct; relentless and always in quest for knowledge.
  • Excellence Striving for unique and exemplary attainment in service delivery; Result and business-oriented.
